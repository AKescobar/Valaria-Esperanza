About

Who is Valaria Esperanza?

Valaria Esperanza is a vtuber based on the lily of the valley flower. She is a flower spirit that is hundreds of years old and is based in a private garden living with other flower spirits.

What is a VTuber?

A vtubers is an online personality who uses a virtual avatar to interact with their audience.

Concepts

Lily of the Valley

"The Return of Happiness"

Valaria is to represent the return of happiness for her viewers as well as herself through a journey. Her journey of improving at video games, coding, and language learning!

Style

Valaria's style is a mixture of various different ones, however it most resembles that of Y2K. Her main outfit consists of a flowy blouse with a spaghetti strap under shirt, and jeans with distressed patching in the shape of the lily of the valley flower. Valaria's aesthetics are similar to her clothing style in that she has a mixture of different aesthetic styles ranging from cyber, gothic, and Y2K, to goblin, fairy, and whimsy cores.

Lore

Valaria is the spirit of a flower, wishing to bring joy where it was lost. Born of a flower watered with the tears of a burdened person, she naturally to took to healing broken hearts. All hearts, but that of the burdened that watered her. They could never fully recover, and Valaria could never undersand why.

Nevertheless The Burdened, her guardian, raised a happy and quickly growing Valaria. within days she had gone from palm-sized to around a yard tall! She could also sing, it was a rich and soothing voice she has, able to melt hearts as well as mend them. She was able to also put the worst of insomiacs to sleep. It was amazing what a girl that came from a grave could do. Besides dancing, that she could and should avoid.

Of course this was all before The Burdened passed away. There was no accident, they simply got gravely ill from drinking a tea that was made from Valaria's flower. No fuss, no muss. This devastated Valaria, she blamed herself, whailed and whailed until her magic voice was gone. Threw herself on The Burdened and clung for dear life, until sedated and taken to a deep part of a nearby forest.

She never knew her flower was poisonous, she never wanted to hurt anyone, she simply wanted to help.
